LOOMIS ARMORED US — EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK v4.1
Last updated: June 2026
Owner: Human Resources Department

SECTION 1: LEAVE POLICY
Full-time employees receive 10 days paid vacation per year.
Vacation days accrue at 0.83 days per month.
Unused vacation days may be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days.
Vacation requests must be submitted 2 weeks in advance.

SECTION 2: SICK LEAVE
Employees receive 5 sick days per calendar year.
Sick days do not carry over to the following year.
Medical documentation is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
Extended illness may qualify for FMLA leave after 3 days.

SECTION 3: UNIFORMS
All field employees must wear full uniform during working hours.
Uniforms are provided by the company at no cost to the employee.
Lost or damaged uniforms must be reported to HR within 24 hours.
Replacement uniforms are provided at company expense for first replacement only.

SECTION 4: CONDUCT
Employees must maintain professional conduct at all times.
Use of personal mobile phones is prohibited during active routes.
Social media posts about operations, routes, or cash amounts are strictly prohibited.
Violations may result in immediate termination.
