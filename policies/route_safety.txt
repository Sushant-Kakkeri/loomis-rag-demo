LOOMIS ARMORED US — ROUTE SAFETY MANUAL v3.0
Last updated: June 2026
Owner: Operations and Security

SECTION 1: WEATHER CONDITIONS
Routes are suspended when visibility drops below 0.5 miles.
Routes are suspended when active flood warnings are in effect for the route area.
Routes are suspended when wind speeds exceed 60mph.
Regional Security Director must approve any route operation during weather warnings.

SECTION 2: VEHICLE REQUIREMENTS
All vehicles must pass daily safety inspection before deployment.
Vehicles with any mechanical fault must be taken out of service immediately.
Fuel level must be above 25% at all times during active routes.
GPS tracking must be active and confirmed before route departure.

SECTION 3: INCIDENT REPORTING
All incidents must be reported to dispatch within 5 minutes of occurrence.
Incident report Form IR-1 must be completed within 2 hours of incident.
Photographs must be taken at the scene if safe to do so.
Driver must not move vehicle until cleared by security team unless safety requires it.

SECTION 4: SUSPICIOUS ACTIVITY
Drivers must report any suspicious following vehicle immediately to dispatch.
Do not attempt to confront or engage suspicious individuals.
If threat is imminent, proceed to nearest police station.
Emergency code BLUE activates immediate law enforcement response.
