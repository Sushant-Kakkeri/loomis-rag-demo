LOOMIS ARMORED US — VAULT ACCESS POLICY v2.3
Last updated: June 2026
Owner: Security Compliance Team

SECTION 1: ACCESS REQUIREMENTS
Vault access requires two authorized personnel at all times.
No exceptions apply for weekends, holidays, or after-hours shifts.
Any deviation must be approved in writing by the Regional Security Director.
Failure to comply may result in immediate termination and criminal prosecution.

SECTION 2: AUTHORIZATION LEVELS
All vault personnel must hold Level 3 clearance or above.
Clearance is reviewed annually by the Security Director.
Temporary clearance may be granted for a maximum of 30 days.
Temporary clearance requires written approval from branch manager and security team.

SECTION 3: CASH LIMITS
Maximum vault cash holding is $50,000 per branch per day.
Any amount exceeding $50,000 requires Regional Director approval.
Cash counts must be verified by both personnel before vault is sealed.
Discrepancies greater than $100 must be reported immediately to the Security Director.

SECTION 4: DOCUMENTATION
Form 7B must be completed before any vault access, no exceptions.
Form 7B must be signed by both authorized personnel.
Completed forms are retained for 7 years per SOX compliance requirements.
Lost or damaged forms must be reported to compliance team within 24 hours.
